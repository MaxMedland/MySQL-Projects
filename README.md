## 🛠 Core Technical Proficiencies

### **Advanced Data Retrieval & Filtering**
* **Precision Extraction:** Expert use of `SELECT`, `DISTINCT`, and `ALIASING` to pull clean, readable datasets from large-scale tables.
* **Complex Logic Filtering:** Utilising `WHERE` clauses combined with `BETWEEN`, `IN`, and `LIKE` pattern matching to isolate specific record cohorts (e.g., high-value orders or regional customer segments).
* **Data Organisation:** Implementing multi-level `ORDER BY` logic to structure output for executive readability and trend identification.

### **Relational Data Architecture (JOINs)**
* **Multi-Table Orchestration:** Proficient in synthesizing data across disparate tables using `INNER`, `LEFT`, and `RIGHT JOINs` to create holistic views of business operations.
* **Schema Navigation:** Experience mapping `Primary Key` to `Foreign Key` relationships to link products, suppliers, customers, and order histories without data loss.
* **Relational Logic:** Applying `CROSS JOINs` and understanding `FULL OUTER JOIN` concepts to analyze exhaustive combinations of data points.

### **Analytical Aggregation & Grouping**
* **Summary Statistics:** Leveraging aggregate functions—`COUNT()`, `SUM()`, `AVG()`, `MIN()`, `MAX()`—to transform thousands of rows into singular performance indicators.
* **Segmented Reporting:** Using `GROUP BY` to perform categorical analysis, such as calculating average population density by country or total sales volume per product category.

### **Database Design & normalisation**
* **Schema Design:** Capable of architecting normalized database structures from scratch, focusing on reducing redundancy and preventing update anomalies.
* **Integrity Constraints:** Implementing one-to-many and many-to-many relationship models while ensuring proper indexing through PK/FK assignments.
